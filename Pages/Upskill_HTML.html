Frequently Asked Questions

What is UpSkill

UpSkill is an online learning community with thousands of classes for creative and curious people, on topics including illustration, design, photography, freelancing, and more. On UpSkill , you’ll find inspiration from hands-on classes and teachers at the top of their creative fields, so you can take the next step in your creative journey.

What can I learn form UpSkill ?

UpSkill has thousands of classes in everything from graphic design to cooking, productivity, entrepreneurship , etc . If it's something creative, you can learn it on UpSkill.

What if I miss the enrollment or classroom open date ?

As soon as you enroll in the program, you can get started! We advertise classroom start dates and project due dates as a general guideline for students, It also helps ensure you join a community of other students who start their learning journey around the same time as you.

Can I cancel my pre-order before payment? How do I cancel?

Yes! You can always cancel your pre-order. For instructions on how to cancel.

What is UpSkill approach to career support ?

Being proactive is one of the most important parts of a job search. Our career services aim to show you how to communicate your newfound skills to the tech industry-- how to format your resume, how to answer technical interview questions, how to update your online profiles on LinkedIn and GitHub, and successfully pursue job leads.

How do I opt out of required career projects?

In the last few years, we have learned from thousands of students and alumni that the most important factor influencing whether students found more opportunities - whether it was collaboration on a cool project or getting interviews - was networking.

What Happen after my Trial is Over?

After your trial ends, your annual UpSkill membership begins. You’ll be billed for the year in full, so you can enjoy continuous access to creative classes year-round.

can I teach on UpSkill?

Yes! UpSkill teachers are everyday creatives and professionals who want to share their passion, and the skills and experience they’ve gained in their creative disciplines with a community of eager learners.

What is Inculding in my UpSkill Membership ?

As a UpSkill member, you’ll have unlimited access to all UpSkill classes to watch when and where you want, and additional features such as offline viewing, access to a vibrant community of lifelong learners, and so much more.

What is Refund and cancellation policy ?

You can cancel and unenroll within the first 2 days from your first month’s subscription purchase date in order to be eligible for a full refund.
